50 Best Free Movies to Stream Right Now in 2026

Streaming subscription fatigue is real. Between Netflix, Disney+, Max, Hulu, Apple TV+, Paramount+, and Peacock, the average household now spends over $80 per month on streaming services. But here is the secret the industry does not want you to know: some of the best movies ever made are available completely free, right now, with zero subscription required.

Ad-supported streaming platforms like Tubi, Pluto TV, Peacock Free, and Plex have quietly assembled massive libraries of critically acclaimed films, cult classics, and blockbusters. All you need is an internet connection. We have curated the definitive list of 50 must-watch free movies across every genre, organized so you can find exactly what you are in the mood for tonight.

Where to Watch for Free: Tubi (largest free library, 50,000+ titles) • Pluto TV (live channels + on-demand) • Peacock Free (NBC content + classics) • Plex (excellent curation) • YouTube Movies (free with ads) • Kanopy (free with library card) • Crackle (Sony films)

Best Free Action & Thriller Movies

The Dark Knight (2008) IMDb 9.0

Christopher Nolan's masterpiece redefined the superhero genre. Heath Ledger's Joker remains one of cinema's greatest performances. Available on Tubi Pluto TV

Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) IMDb 8.1

George Miller's relentless, visually stunning action epic. Won 6 Academy Awards. A near-perfect action film. Available on Tubi Plex

John Wick (2014) IMDb 7.4

The film that reinvented action choreography. Keanu Reeves at his most iconic. The franchise that launched a thousand imitators. Available on Peacock Free Tubi

Heat (1995) IMDb 8.3

Michael Mann's crime epic featuring the legendary first on-screen meeting of Al Pacino and Robert De Niro. A 3-hour masterclass in tension. Available on Tubi

The Raid (2011) IMDb 7.6

The Indonesian action film that set a new global standard for hand-to-hand combat choreography. Relentlessly intense from start to finish. Available on Tubi Plex

Best Free Drama Movies

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) IMDb 9.3

Consistently ranked the greatest film ever made on IMDb. A story of hope, friendship, and perseverance that never loses its power. Available on Tubi Pluto TV

Parasite (2019) IMDb 8.5

Bong Joon-ho's Oscar-winning masterpiece about class inequality. Brilliantly shifts genres mid-film. The first non-English film to win Best Picture. Available on Tubi

Schindler's List (1993) IMDb 9.0

Spielberg's devastating Holocaust drama. One of the most important films ever made. Won 7 Academy Awards including Best Picture. Available on Peacock Free

Moonlight (2016) IMDb 7.4

Barry Jenkins' intimate, visually poetic coming-of-age story. Best Picture winner. One of the most beautifully shot films of the decade. Available on Tubi Plex

Best Free Comedy Movies

Superbad (2007) IMDb 7.6

The definitive coming-of-age comedy of the 2000s. Jonah Hill and Michael Cera at their absolute best. Endlessly quotable. Available on Tubi Crackle

The Grand Budapest Hotel (2014) IMDb 8.1

Wes Anderson's most accessible and delightful film. A visually stunning, whimsical adventure with an all-star cast. Available on Tubi Plex

Game Night (2018) IMDb 7.0

A criminally underrated comedy thriller. Jason Bateman and Rachel McAdams are perfect together. Genuinely funny and surprisingly clever. Available on Tubi Peacock Free

Best Free Horror Movies

Get Out (2017) IMDb 7.7

Jordan Peele's debut is a genre-defining social horror film. Brilliantly crafted, deeply unsettling, and culturally significant. Available on Tubi Peacock Free

Hereditary (2018) IMDb 7.3

Ari Aster's debut is one of the most genuinely terrifying films in decades. Toni Collette delivers a career-best performance. Not for the faint of heart. Available on Tubi

The Witch (2015) IMDb 6.9

Robert Eggers' slow-burn folk horror set in 1630s New England. Atmospheric, historically authentic, and deeply unsettling. Available on Tubi Plex

Best Free Sci-Fi Movies

Interstellar (2014) IMDb 8.7

Christopher Nolan's emotionally devastating space epic. Scientifically grounded, visually breathtaking, and genuinely moving. Hans Zimmer's score is transcendent. Available on Tubi Pluto TV

Arrival (2016) IMDb 7.9

Denis Villeneuve's cerebral first-contact film. Amy Adams is extraordinary. One of the most intellectually satisfying sci-fi films ever made. Available on Tubi Plex

Ex Machina (2014) IMDb 7.7

A tense, claustrophobic AI thriller that feels more relevant every year. Alicia Vikander's performance as Ava is haunting. Available on Tubi Peacock Free

Best Free Documentary Movies

Free Solo (2018) IMDb 8.2

Alex Honnold's attempt to free-solo climb El Capitan. The most anxiety-inducing documentary ever made. Oscar winner for Best Documentary. Available on Tubi YouTube

The Social Dilemma (2020) IMDb 7.6

Former tech insiders expose how social media platforms are engineered to manipulate behavior. Essential viewing for anyone with a smartphone. Available on Tubi Plex

13th (2016) IMDb 8.2

Ava DuVernay's powerful examination of racial inequality in the US criminal justice system. One of the most important documentaries of the decade. Available on YouTube (free, official)

Best Free Animated Movies

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018) IMDb 8.4

Arguably the greatest animated film ever made. Groundbreaking visual style, emotionally resonant story, and a perfect superhero origin. Available on Tubi Pluto TV

Spirited Away (2001) IMDb 8.6

Hayao Miyazaki's Oscar-winning masterpiece. A richly imaginative fantasy that works on every level for every age. Available on Tubi

Pro Tip: Install the free Tubi or Plex app on your smart TV, Roku, Fire Stick, or Apple TV for the best viewing experience. Both apps are completely free, require no credit card, and have better interfaces than their mobile counterparts. Tubi alone has over 50,000 movies and TV shows — more than any paid streaming service.

The era of paying for every piece of content you watch is over. Between Tubi, Pluto TV, Plex, Peacock Free, and YouTube, you have access to thousands of hours of world-class cinema at absolutely no cost. Bookmark this list, pick a genre, and enjoy your movie night — guilt-free.
