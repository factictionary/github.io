1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve website functionality and enhance your browsing experience. Factictionary.in uses cookies for analytics and personalized advertising through Google services.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To analyze traffic and understand how visitors use the site.
  • To serve relevant ads through Google AdSense.
  • To maintain your preferences and improve site performance.

3. Managing Cookies

You can accept or decline cookies by modifying your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features but will not prevent access to our tools.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our advertising partners may also use cookies to deliver tailored content. You can learn more about Google’s cookie use in Google’s Advertising Policy.
