Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Factictionary (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates https://factictionary.in. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our website.

1. Data Privacy Commitment

Factictionary.in is designed as a browser-based platform. All tools run directly within your browser, and we do not collect, transmit, or store any user files or data on our servers. Your activity remains private and local to your device.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect minimal non-personal information for analytics or advertising purposes, such as browser type or approximate location, through Google AdSense or Google Analytics, which use cookies to serve relevant ads.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our site uses cookies solely to improve user experience and deliver personalized ads in compliance with Google Publisher Policies. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services like Google AdSense and YouTube embeds. These services may use their own cookies or tracking mechanisms as per their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure a safe browsing experience. Since our tools operate client-side, your files never leave your device.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at factictionary@gmail.com.
